Data Professional Survey Analysis

The primary aim of this project is to provide a comprehensive analysis of survey data collected from data professionals. The project seeks to understand the key factors influencing the careers of data professionals, including salary, job satisfaction, and the challenges faced when entering the field. Additionally, the project explores the most popular programming languages among these professionals and provides demographic insights into the respondents' geographical distribution and age. By visualizing these insights, the project aims to support career planning and decision-making for individuals considering or currently working in data-related roles.
